Humba


Description

Humba is a dish composed of pork and spices with a dark and rich sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly or Pork Hock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Pepper
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients in a pot.
  2. Let it simmer until pork meat and fat becomes soft.
  3. Reduce the sauce until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Serve it warm and enjoy.
