Bicol Express


Description

Bicol Express is a dish composed of coconut milk, a meat product and chilies. Its a savory and spicy dish.

Ingredients

  • Meat Product: Pork, Chicken, Beef
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chilies
  • Spices: Ginger, onion, garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Sautee spices in hot oil until fragrant.
  2. Add meat and sautee until there is browning on the meat.
  3. Add half coconut milk and chillies and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining coconut milk and sautee for 15 seconds.
  5. Serve it warm and enjoy.
